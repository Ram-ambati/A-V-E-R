How it works

Our cutting-edge Audio Visual Emotion Recognition (A V E R) system leverages the power of two advanced CNN models to analyze and interpret human emotions:

Face Model

The face model captures and analyzes facial expressions from images and video. By detecting subtle changes in facial muscles, our system can accurately interpret a wide range of emotions. Whether it's a smile, frown, or raised eyebrow, the face model decodes these expressions to provide a detailed emotional analysis.

Audio Model

The audio model processes vocal tones and speech patterns from audio recordings. By examining pitch, tone, rhythm, and other vocal characteristics, the model can identify emotions conveyed through speech. This analysis helps us understand the emotional context behind the words, providing a deeper insight into the speaker's feelings.
